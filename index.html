Methodology

I work by exploring and validating problems, ideating a range of potential solutions, validating that: value, usability, technical feasibility and business viability risks are managed. This ensures my team can get maximum innovation from discovery whilst assuring that developers know what they're building, and why and that they have the space and solid plans to build at production quality.

Having been creating software products for over twenty years I have seen that the product methodologies described by the silicon valley product group (SVPG) are our industry's best practice.

I drive teams that follow lean and agile practices or help your organisation transform to follow these methods so that you can acheive maximum outcomes for your investment, to delight your customers and reveal new opportunities.
